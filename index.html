Welcome

Ever since the early 2000's, electronic devices have become
more popular, advanced, and involved in our lives

This came with a increased focus on protection and interactions with others online
which is why the Cyber Chip was created in 2017 with the goal of teaching scouts these topics

This website will guide you through the requirements and knowledge needed
to earn your Cyber Chip and be internet-smart in the future

Requirements:

  1. Read and sign the Level II Internet Safety Pledge
  2. Write and sign a personalized contract with your parent or guardian
    that outlines rules for using the computer and mobile devices
  3. Watch 3 NetSmartz videos including "Friend or Fake?"
  4. Discuss the acceptable standards and practices for using allowed devices
    at your meetings and other Scouting events
  5. Use the EDGE method to teach Internet safety rules, behavior, and “netiquette”

Level II Internet Safety Pledge
